ABOUT PERFECTHIRE GLOBAL

A revenue recruitment boutique, run by operators.

PerfectHire Global is the successor to PerfectHire.nl, founded by Ralph Kazen. We do one thing: place revenue talent for SaaS, tech and AI companies across the Benelux and the US.

THE STORY

Recruitment that is truly personal.

PerfectHire was founded by Ralph Kazen on a simple conviction: recruitment can and should be better. Not sending more candidates, but finding the right one. Not taking on more clients, but truly serving the ones you do.

We are fully dedicated to sales in the SaaS and IT sector, from inside sales to VP of Sales. That focus has given us a deep international network and market knowledge a generalist agency simply cannot match.
